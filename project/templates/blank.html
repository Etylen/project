The topic of the class

Theme 1. Introduction to the specialty. Features inspection trauma and orthopedic patients. Modern principles of fracture. Damage to the shoulder girdle.

 

Theme 2. Traumatic dislocations. Damage to the ligaments, tendons and muscles. Damage of bones and joints of the upper limb. Damage of the spine.

 

Theme 3. Amputation of limbs. Rehabilitation and Prosthetics disabled people with limb defects. Treating trauma and orthopedic patients in an outpatient setting.

Theme 4. Damage of pelvis. Damages bones and joints of the lower limbs. Imposition of skeletal extract and external fixators.

Theme 5. Traumatic disease. Polytrauma. Crush syndrome. Traumatic shock. Gunshot wounds bones and joints.

Theme 6. Degenerative diseases of the spine and joints. Inflammatory, tumor and tumor diseases of support and movement.

 

Theme.7. Inborn and dysplastic diseases of bones and joints. Scoliosis. Inflammatory, tumor and tumor diseases of support and movement.
